How a transition to retirement account could work for you

A transition to retirement account (or TTR) pays you an income from your super savings while you continue to work, so you can ease into retirement on your terms.

Key points:
  • You can start a transition to retirement account any time after you’ve reached your preservation age (between 58 and 64)
  • It’s a smart way to put your money to work and you’ll save on tax
  • You can work less without having less money in your pocket
